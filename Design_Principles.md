# Design Principles

## Identity Loop

Identity influences expectation.  
Expectation influences behavior.  
Behavior influences results.  
Results reinforce identity.

---

## Conditioning Mechanism

Repetition leads to conditioning.  
Conditioning leads to certainty.  
Certainty influences action.

---

## Expectation Dynamics

Expectation stabilizes emotional state.  
Worry introduces conflicting signals.

---

## Responsibility Principle

Responsibility enables change.  
Lack of responsibility prevents change.

---

## Attention–Intention Relationship

Attention directs energy.  
Intention influences direction of outcome.

---

## Environmental Influence

Input influences thought patterns.  
Thought patterns influence emotional state.  
Emotional state influences behavior.

---

## Consistency Effect

Consistent action produces compounding results.  
Inconsistent action prevents identity stabilization.
